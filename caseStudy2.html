Prayer Hall Management: Website

Project description

UX Case Study

Duration: 14th Febuary - 25th May

Product Overview

This case study delves into the designing and creation of a user-friendly website that enables users to get information about their religious well-being and where the prayer councilors can keep track of the prayer hall events.


Problem

Difficult to manage and keep track of all the events related to prayer hall: Users struggled to manage and maintain tracks of events systematically.


Difficult to keep records of absentees: Users find it difficult to maintain records of absentees and the collected fines from the absentees.


Goal

Develop a website that helps users to maintain and manage all the prayer hall related events systematically.

Roles and Responsibilities

My Role

UI/UX Designer: Responsible for the overall design and user experience for Prayer Hall Management website.

Team

My responsibility involves conducting user research, designing wireframes, developing the website's features, testing and iterating, and implementing the final design and functionality.

Usability study: findings

In my usability studies for the prayer hall management website , I conducted research to gather valuable insights from users. The insights revealed challenges and numerous parameters like Navigation efficiency and User satisfaction.

Findings

  • Users were confused about the navigation of the website and suggested a clear navigation structure for the website.
  • While users appreciated the quality of the content, they recommended enhancing readability by using larger fonts and more visual aids..
  • Feedback highlighted the need for more interactive elements to enhance user engagement.

Accessibility

Color Contrast

  • Ensured sufficient color contrast between text and background elements to improve readability and accommodate users with visual impairments.

    • Consistent layout and Navigation

  • The design maintains a consistent and intuitive layout, aiding users with cognitive disabilities in navigating and using the platform without confusion.

    • Screen Reader Compatibility

  • Ensured proper labeling of interface elements and utilized ARIA attributes to optimize compatibility with screen readers, enhancing accessibility for visually impaired users

    • Takeaways

    Impact

  • Ensured proper labeling of interface elements and utilized ARIA attributes to optimize compatibility with screen readers, enhancing accessibility for visually impaired users
